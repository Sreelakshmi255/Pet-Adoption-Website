Pet Adoption Terms & Conditions

Adoption Eligibility: Applicants must be at least 18 years old and capable of providing a safe and loving environment.

Application Process: All adoption applications are subject to review and approval by our team.

Pet Care Responsibility: Adopters agree to provide adequate food, shelter, medical care, and affection to their adopted pet.

Return Policy: If an adopter is unable to care for the pet, they must contact us for assistance in rehoming.

Privacy & Data Usage: Personal details provided during the adoption process are securely stored and will not be shared without consent.

Legal Agreement: By adopting a pet, applicants agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Contact: For any questions regarding adoption, please reach out to us at support@petadoption.com.
